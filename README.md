This is a basic walk-in health self-check kiosk online app. Users enter their name, age, sex, weight, and height, and the app calculates their BMI and presents a color-coded result card with their health category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese) along with a short advice message and a visual gauge displaying where their BMI falls. Every contribution is also logged to a shared Google Sheet using Google Apps Script, allowing staff to track usage and follow up with anyone at risk. The interface is made of plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and it is fully responsive, meaning it works well on desktop, tablet, and mobile displays.
